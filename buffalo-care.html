Buffalo Care: Feeding, Housing, Behaviour & Common Diseases

For beginners and farmers • Reading time: 10–12 minutes

Buffaloes are strong and gentle animals commonly raised for milk, meat, and farm work. Proper care ensures healthy animals and better productivity.

Feeding Buffalo

A balanced diet includes green fodder, dry roughage, grains, and clean water. Mineral supplements are also important.

Housing

Provide a clean, dry, and ventilated shelter. Buffaloes like cool environments and access to water for bathing.

Behaviour

Buffaloes are calm animals but need gentle handling. Stress can reduce milk yield.

Common Diseases

Foot and mouth disease, mastitis, and parasites are common. Regular vaccination and hygiene help prevent illness.
